
## AGI Assembly Application: User Guide

Welcome to the AGI Assembly application! This guide will help you understand how to use the application effectively to meet your needs in legal, medical, and crisis management domains.

---

### Key Features

1. **Advanced Document Summarization**  
   - Upload lengthy reports, legal documents, academic papers, or technical manuals.  
   - Receive concise summaries tailored to your preferences (e.g., executive summaries, bullet-point lists, or detailed abstracts).  
   - Focus on specific sections, such as methodology, conclusions, or recommendations, for targeted insights.

2. **Real-Time Insights and Updates**  
   - Extract actionable insights from reports or datasets.  
   - Stay updated with real-time changes, such as new legal rulings, updated research findings, or revised policy documents.

3. **Scenario-Based Analysis**  
   - Use predictive modeling to explore potential outcomes for legal cases, medical treatments, or crisis scenarios.  
   - Receive recommendations based on evidence and trends.

4. **Multilingual Support**  
   - Upload documents in various languages and receive accurate summaries or analyses in your preferred language.  
   - Adapt content for cross-cultural relevance, ensuring regional or local considerations are addressed.

5. **Ethical and Legal Compliance**  
   - Ensure all outputs comply with ethical standards, copyright laws, and intellectual property guidelines.  
   - Validate sources and cross-reference data for accuracy and reliability.

---

### How to Use the Application

1. **Getting Started**  
   - Log in to the AGI Assembly application using your credentials.  
   - Navigate to the dashboard to access the main features.

2. **Uploading Documents**  
   - Click on the "Upload" button and select your document (supported formats: PDF, Word, scanned images).  
   - Specify your preferences for summarization or analysis (e.g., focus on legal rulings, scientific findings, or policy recommendations).

3. **Customizing Outputs**  
   - Choose the level of detail for summaries:  
     - High-level overview  
     - Detailed breakdown  
     - Bullet-point format  
   - Highlight specific sections or topics for a more focused analysis.

4. **Accessing Real-Time Updates**  
   - Enable notifications to receive updates on changes to legal rulings, research findings, or policy documents.  
   - Use the "Live Updates" feature to track ongoing developments in your area of interest.

5. **Scenario-Based Analysis**  
   - Input your scenario details (e.g., legal case parameters, medical conditions, or crisis variables).  
   - Review predictive models and recommendations generated by the application.

6. **Multilingual Summarization**  
   - Upload documents in your preferred language.  
   - Select the output language for summaries or analyses.  
   - Review content adapted for cross-cultural relevance.

### Best Practices

- **Ask Specific Questions**: When using the application for research or analysis, provide clear and specific queries to receive focused results.  
- **Validate Sources**: Cross-check outputs with trusted sources to ensure accuracy and reliability.  
- **Stay Updated**: Regularly check for updates to ensure you are working with the latest information.  
- **Respect Ethical Guidelines**: Use the application responsibly, adhering to fair use principles and intellectual property laws.

---

### Troubleshooting

- **Issue: Document Upload Fails**  
   - Ensure the file format is supported (PDF, Word, or image).  
   - Check your internet connection and try again.  

- **Issue: Inaccurate Summaries**  
   - Refine your input preferences to focus on specific sections or topics.  
   - Contact support if the issue persists.

- **Issue: Real-Time Updates Not Working**  
   - Verify that notifications are enabled in your settings.  
   - Ensure your subscription includes the "Live Updates" feature.
